01 / RESEARCH APPROACH

How I approach biological evidence.

My goal is to connect computational results into a transparent evidence chain while distinguishing statistical association from biological causation.

01

REPRODUCIBILITY

Validate beyond one cohort.

I compare discovery results with an independent dataset whenever possible, reducing the chance that a finding reflects only one sample collection or platform.

02

INTEGRATION

Study multiple evidence layers.

Expression, pathway context, mutation frequency, and clinical outcomes answer different questions. Their agreement—or disagreement—creates a more informative interpretation.

03

SCIENTIFIC CAUTION

Make claims the data can support.

Retrospective associations can nominate biomarkers and hypotheses, but functional experiments and prospective studies are still needed before clinical conclusions.
